1. Overview

1.1 What is this system?

The MXR India Daily Reporting System is a web-based tool that enables every team member to log their daily work activities, and gives management a real-time view of team progress, blockers, and project status.

Key purposes of this system:

  • Transparency ŌĆö Everyone can see what the team is working on each day
  • Accountability ŌĆö A clear record of who submitted reports and who didn't
  • Knowledge sharing ŌĆö Blockers and plans are visible so colleagues can help
  • Reporting ŌĆö Monthly data is archived and downloadable as Excel for HQ reporting

📌 Who uses this system?

All employees submit daily reports and view the Dashboard.
Admin users additionally manage accounts, approve new signups, and access user controls.

2.1 Signing Up

If you are a new employee and do not yet have an account, follow these steps to register:

  2. On the Login page, click the "Sign Up" link at the bottom.
  3. Enter your Full Name (this is how you will appear in reports).

4. Daily Reports ŌĆö Submit Your Daily Work

The Daily Reports tab is where you submit your work activity at the end of each working day. This is the most frequently used feature of the system.

4.3 Submitting

  1. Fill in all required fields (marked ).
  2. Review your Accomplishments, Blockers, and Tomorrow's Plan.
  3. Click the "Submit Task" button.
  4. Wait for the loading indicator to finish (a few seconds).
  5. You will be automatically redirected to the Dashboard where your report will now appear.

📌 Submitting Multiple Reports in One Day

You can submit more than one report per day if you worked on multiple distinct projects. Simply go to Daily Reports again and submit another entry for the same date with the other project details.

ŌÜĀ’ĖÅ No Edit After Submission

Once submitted, a report cannot be edited. If you made a serious mistake, contact your administrator. Always review your entry before clicking Submit.

The live timeline shows the past 90 days of data. Older data is moved to Monthly Archives automatically at the end of each month. Use the archive download to access data older than 90 days.

7. Admin Panel (Admin Users Only)

🔒 Restricted Access

The Admin panel is only visible and accessible to users with the Admin role. Regular employees will not see this tab. If you believe you should have admin access, contact your manager.

7.1 Approving New Users

When a new employee signs up, their account is placed in a "Pending" state. The admin must approve them before they can access the system.

  1. Click "Admin" in the sidebar.
  2. Look at the "Pending Approval" section at the top of the page.
  3. Review each pending user ŌĆö their name, email, and registration date are shown.
  4. Click "Approve" to grant access, OR click "Reject" to delete the registration request.

📌 After Approval

Once approved, the user immediately moves from "Pending" to the "User Management" list and can log in without any further action.

8.2 Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I signed up but cannot log in. What should I do?

Your account is pending admin approval. Contact your manager or administrator and ask them to approve your account in the Admin panel.

Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?

Contact your administrator and ask them to reset your password. They will reset it to 111111. Log in with that and you will be able to use the system.

Q: I submitted the wrong information. Can I edit my report?

No ŌĆö submitted reports cannot be edited. Contact your administrator to explain the situation. For record-keeping purposes, you may also submit a corrective note in tomorrow's report mentioning the error.

Q: Can I submit a report for a previous day?

Yes. On the Daily Reports form, change the Task Date field to the date you want to report for. This is useful if you forgot to submit the day before.

Q: My project is not in the dropdown. What do I do?

Select "Other / Enter manually" at the bottom of the project dropdown and type the project name. Alternatively, go to the Projects tab and add the project there ŌĆö it will then appear for everyone in the dropdown.

Q: How do I know if I have submitted today?

Check the Dashboard ŌĆö your name should appear in the "Submitted" panel. If your name is in the "Not Submitted" panel, you still need to submit for today.

Q: Where can I download past reports for HQ reporting?

Go to Work History and find the Monthly Archive card for the relevant month. Click "Download Excel" to download the full month's data.

Q: How is the system accessed? Is there an app?

The system runs in any modern web browser (Chrome recommended). You can also install it as a desktop app (PWA) by clicking the "Add to Desktop" button ŌĆö this creates an app icon that opens the system directly without needing to open a browser first.

Q: What should I write in Accomplishments if I had a very slow day?

Be honest and specific. Even on slow days, write what you did: attended a meeting, reviewed documentation, investigated an issue, helped a colleague, etc. The system is meant to give an accurate picture of how time is being spent ŌĆö there are no wrong answers as long as they are truthful.
